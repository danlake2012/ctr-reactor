CTR Reactor — Campaign Performance Insights

CTR Reactor helps advertisers understand engagement and click‑through trends by analyzing Google Ads performance metrics (impressions, clicks, cost, CTR) to generate optimization suggestions. Access is limited to authorized Google Ads accounts; data is never resold.

Use of Google Ads API

CTR Reactor retrieves aggregate campaign statistics from the Google Ads API for the account owner’s authorized properties. We use these metrics to power internal dashboards and optimization recommendations. No third‑party data sharing.

Scopes & data: ad performance metrics (e.g., impressions, clicks, cost, CTR). Tokens are stored securely server‑side.

Privacy & Data Use

We only access data for accounts that explicitly grant consent via OAuth. Data is used for reporting and optimization within CTR Reactor and is not sold or transferred. Users may revoke access at any time.

Contact us for data inquiries or revocation.
